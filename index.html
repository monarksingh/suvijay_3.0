ABOUT US

History

Our journey started as a Proprietary Organization in 1984 by a young Civil Engineer, Mr. Jawahar Bhandari and later became a Partnership Organization in 1989. The Organization was also active as Builders and Developers under the name SBL Constructions till 1993. Keeping the growing business in mind and visualizing the strength of the Organization as also realizing the growth potential in the 80’s & 90’s in the Industrial and Institutional sectors, the Organization decided to target that segment to emerge as a trusted name in Civil Contracting. Crossing the borders of the home state, Suvijay ventured into a large scale Institutional Project of the proportion of a Deemed University, at Bhuj in Kutch, post the earthquake. This led to establishment of the Gujarat Division and formation of Suvijay Buildcon (Pune) Pvt. Ltd. (SBPPL) in the year 2007.

COMPANY

  • SBPPL is a professionally managed Construction Company with a strong presence in the States of Maharashtra and Gujarat for the past 30 years
  • SBPPL has carved a niche for itself by executing many prestigious Industrial, Institutional and Residential Projects to the full satisfaction of the clients who include some of the well-known MNC’s and leading Indian Corporate Houses.
  • SBPPL is today in a position to boast of a Premier Construction Company with its team of dedicated and motivated engineers, supervisors, skilled workers, subcontractors, office staff and reliable suppliers. We today have a committed labour force in excess of 2000 men working at different sites.
  • The Institutional Projects executed by us have created a benchmark in terms of quality and budgeted execution.
  • Axioum, the interior design and execution company from the Suvijay Group, with its team can offer end-to-end solutions and take projects on turnkey basis under one single window.

Current Scenario

Suvijay Buildcon (Pune) Pvt. Ltd. (SBPPL) is an ISO 9001 - 2008 & OSHAS 18001 – 2007 Company in the field of Civil Construction, operational over a period spanning three decades. Today SBPPL ranks as the first-amongst-equals in its core business of Civil Construction in different sectors. The success mantra is based on commitment to quality, timely delivery and no cost over-run.
